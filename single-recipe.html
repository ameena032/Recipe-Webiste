Fluffy Banana Pancakes

Indulge in these fluffy banana pancakes that are not only delicious but also super easy to whip up! Perfect for breakfast or brunch, they will delight both kids and adults alike. Top with syrup, honey, or fresh fruit for an unforgettable treat!

Prep Time

30 min

Cook Time

15 min

Serving

6 servings

Tags : Breakfast Pancakes Food

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Batter

In a large mixing bowl, combine the pancake mix and flax seed meal. Gradually whisk in the skim milk until the batter is smooth and free of lumps. Let it rest for a few minutes while you heat the skillet.

Step 2: Heat the Skillet

Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease the pan with cooking spray or a bit of butter. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake, allowing space between them.

Step 3: Cook to Perfection

Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2-3 minutes. Flip carefully and cook for another 2-3 minutes until golden brown. Keep warm in a low oven while you repeat with the remaining batter.

Step 4: Serve & Enjoy

Stack the pancakes high and serve warm with your favorite toppings: syrup, sliced bananas, berries, or a dollop of whipped cream for a special touch!

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups pancake mix

1 tbsp flax seed meal

1 1/4 cups skim milk

1 tbsp butter, melted

1 banana, mashed

Cooking spray

Tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
